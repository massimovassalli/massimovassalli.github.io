Fees

In our effort to keep low prices for the conference, we identified three fee packages. Nevertheless, we suggest all attendees to evaluate the full package, as it gives access to all the meals of the conference (4 lunches, 2 dinners) thus maximising the opportunities of networking and engagement with the speakers and the other participants. All packages include the access to the conference and the coffee breaks. Note that prices are different for shared or single room accommodation and fees are for early bird registration.
