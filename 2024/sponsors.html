Sponsors

Leading companies are supporting the symposium "Nanoengineering for Mechanobiology". They not only provide a financial help to organise the event, but constitute an important asset of the conference. The audience of N4M is composed of passionate scientists, intrigued by adopting cutting edge technological approaches to investigate biological systems at different scales, integrating structure, composition and mechanics. N4M sponsors always offer a breath of what is achievable in this area using their technology and service.

Bruker

Bruker BioAFM, a world-leading manufacturer of Atomic Force Microscopes and Optical Tweezers, delivers renowned, high-performance instrumentation and analytical solutions.

Visit website »

ICAPPIC

ICAPPIC is a manufacturer of nanoanalytical instruments - particularly scanning ion conductance microscope (SICM) systems and related equipment - for a broad range of applications related to electrophysiology, ion-channel pharmacology and other biomedical applications.

Visit website »

Nanosurf

Nanosurf will show never before seen high-end capabilities with the new DriveAFM platform. It integrates with FluidFM technology, allows mass measurements of single living cells, features unparalleled imaging stability in liquid, and more. Visit us at our booth to get the full picture of the future of AFM.

Visit website »

Impetux

Our SENSOCELL optical tweezers offer extended manipulation capabilities over multiple traps (up to 256) and simultaneous force measurements without requiring any previous calibration, even inside living cells or tissues. SENSOCELL enables measuring forces in vitro and in vivo in a direct, absolute, and non-invasive way by trapping microspheres, bacteria and cells as a whole or even endogenous subcellular structures like vesicles.

Visit website »

CellSense

CellSense is a pioneer in advanced microscopy solutions, focusing on non-invasive techniques that reveal the mechanical properties of cells, tissues, and whole organisms without perturbing or contacting the samples. We specialize in Brillouin Microscopy, an optical technique that supports a broad range of applications in areas such as mechanobiology, 3D tissue models, and disease research.

Visit website »

MMI

Molecular Machines & Industries (MMI) is the leading provider of microscopic single-cell isolation and imaging tools. We specialize in cell picking of rare cells, laser microdissection, whole slide imaging and optical tweezers for a wide range of clinical and research-based applications in life sciences, material sciences, and healthcare. Our team of passionate researchers help scientists around the world to achieve their research objectives by providing them with the tools they need to succeed.

Visit website »

Schaefer Italy

Schaefer SEE is a microscopy services company active in Italy since 2005. We bring to market highly innovative nano-scale characterization instruments. We offer premium after-sale customer support delivered by our trained engineers and test and measurement services based on our panel of instruments.
