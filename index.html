Nanoengineering for Mechanobiology - N4M

This is the home for the "Nanoengineering for Mechanobiology" symposium, a recurrent event being organized in early spring each year to bring together, in an informal context, great scientists from all over the world, interested in discussing about mechanobiology and meeting colleagues and companies addressing scientific questions and developing dedicated technology.
