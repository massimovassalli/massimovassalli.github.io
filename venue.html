Nanoengineering for Mechanobiology - The Venue

Nanoengineering for Mechanobiology (N4M) was born in Camogli (Genova, Italy) in 2016 (see the history), organized within the premises of the hotel Cenobio dei Dogi. The success of the event suggested to adopt this as the elected venue for N4M.

Camogli is a small fishermen village on the Italian coast, south of the historic city of Genova, at the door of the park comprising the famous locations of Portofino and Cinque Terre. The hotel Cenobio dei Dogi is at walk distance from the Camogli train station (but everything there is close by) which is linked to Genova main station and Genova airport (45-60 min trip, respectively).
